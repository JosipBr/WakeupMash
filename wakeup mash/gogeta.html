SSJ4 GOGETA

The strongest fusion warrior finally joins the DBFZ roster. SSJ4 Gogeta is a heavy rushdown character who just wants to be in your opponents face at all time. He excels at close range with his huge normals. He also has tools that help him in his neutral gameplay. He is one of the two characters who has double air dash. He also has a unique taunt mechanic which gives him one level up boost with his Finsih Sign. At level 7 he can preform and upgraded x100 Big Bang Kamekameha which one shots your opponent. His moveset originates from the TV show and other games asswell.
